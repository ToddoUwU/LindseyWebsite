
Title
	Pink Moon Mothra

ArtDescription 
	Acrylic portrait from series about aspects of womanhood featuring women personally influential in the life of the artist, and represented as various anthropomorphic types of insects and other bugs. This portrait of the sacred spirituality of motherhood features the artist and her two sons as a moth and caterpillars, against an abstract night sky and blooming sacred datura flowers.
